RRLL Draft Recap – Season 2

The RRLL Season 2 Draft has officially concluded! With team owners securing their dream rosters, this draft was full of surprising picks, strategic decisions, and big names. Let's dive into the highlights and recap the major moves.

Draft Headlines & Highlights

🔥 Florida Flamingos Build Around MiniVIP
The Flamingos kicked things off by selecting MiniVIP with the first overall pick, cementing their offensive line. They continued to bolster their squad with strong picks like Fentanyl OBJ, KingKhrist, and Siah.
Summary: The Flamingos focused on high-scoring players to build a powerhouse offense.

⚓ Navy Sailors Secure Versatile Talent
The Sailors took Mix with their second overall pick, focusing on versatility and depth. They followed up by adding key players like Lurk, ThatOneKingGuy, and MrWorldWide to create a well-rounded team.
Summary: The Sailors made strategic picks for a versatile and adaptable roster.

🦟 Montgomery Mosquitos Go Defensive and Deep
The Mosquitos selected Altitude with the third overall pick, focusing on strong defensive play. They further solidified their roster with picks like Cody, 5StarMax, and Pink Grandma.
Summary: The Mosquitos stacked up on defense and veteran leadership.

🐾 Capybaras Add High-Value Talent
The Capybaras selected Tbestg early on, followed by some key picks like MikeEvans, Tipy, and Crazy!—all proven players with immense value.
Summary: The Capybaras brought in experienced and skilled players to strengthen their team.
